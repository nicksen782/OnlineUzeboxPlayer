Stormforce
----------

A vertical shooter:

Use your "Stormforce"-star fighter to fight your way through 4 levels.
There are no shields, so use your 3 smartbombs and 3 lives carefully.



Features:
	- 4 levels
	- some enemies
	- some sound effects


Controls:
Your fighter is controlled with controller 1. 
Use d-pad to move your fighter, press A for single laser or B for double laser fire.
Pressing Y or SELECT will trigger one of your 3 smart bombs.
Use START to pause the game.
